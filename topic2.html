Mumble Rappers Lyrical Rappers About Home

Lyrical rap is a style that most older rappers or “old heads” use, where the lyrics don’t come off like a mumble, it’s not too repetitive, and understandable. With this skill some rappers even put punchlines into the lyrics to make the song even better and make the artist sound talented. Some Rappers that can be called "old heads" are J cole, Kendrick Lamar, J cole, A Tribe Called Quest, Logic, Joyner Lucas and much more.
